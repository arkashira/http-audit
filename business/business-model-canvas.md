# Business Model Canvas for HTTP-Audit

## Customer Segments
- System administrators managing Linux servers.
- Security professionals focused on HTTP server security.
- Organizations requiring compliance with security standards (e.g., PCI-DSS, HIPAA).
- Enterprises with large-scale web applications needing detailed monitoring.

## Value Propositions
- Real-time monitoring of HTTP server interactions at the kernel level.
- Anomaly detection capabilities to identify unusual patterns in server traffic.
- Enhanced security posture by providing insights into potential vulnerabilities.
- Easy integration with existing Linux-based infrastructure.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the Axentx website.
- Partnerships with cybersecurity firms for bundled offerings.
- Online marketplaces (e.g., GitHub, AWS Marketplace) for wider reach.
- Technical webinars and workshops to demonstrate product capabilities.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer support via email and chat.
- Community forums for user engagement and feedback.
- Regular updates and feature enhancements based on user input.
- Training sessions for enterprise clients to maximize product usage.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: $29/month (approx. 1,000 THB) per user.
- Enterprise licensing: $1,500/year (approx. 50,000 THB) for organizations with up to 50 servers.
- Consulting services for implementation and customization: $150/hour (approx. 5,000 THB).
- Premium support packages for critical infrastructure: $500/month (approx. 17,000 THB).

## Key Resources
- Development team with expertise in Linux kernel and security.
- Existing Axentx infrastructure for product deployment and support.
- Marketing resources for outreach and customer acquisition.
- Community of users for feedback and continuous improvement.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and improvement of the HTTP-Audit tool.
- Conducting market research to stay ahead of security trends.
- Building partnerships with cybersecurity firms and platforms.
- Providing customer support and training to enhance user experience.

## Key Partners
- Cybersecurity firms for joint marketing and product bundling.
- Open-source communities for collaboration and feedback.
- Cloud service providers for integration and distribution.
- Compliance organizations for aligning product features with industry standards.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: salaries for engineers and product managers.
- Marketing expenses for outreach and promotional activities.
- Customer support costs for maintaining service quality.
- Infrastructure costs for hosting and maintaining the product.
